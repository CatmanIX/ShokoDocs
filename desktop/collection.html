Collection Tab

The Focus Of Shoko Desktop

The Collection tab is the main focus on Shoko Desktop and allows you to view, modify and update each series in your collection. It's also one of the few tabs packed with settings and information and such has been divided into two parts, Filter Overview and Collection Overview.

Filter Overview

The first thing you’ll notice after clicking Collection from the Main Menu is the two-column layout which we’ll cover in just a minute. Below the Main Menu is the Filter Toolbar which allows you to easily navigate and organize your collection. Below the Filter Toolbar we see the two-column layout previously mentioned, the left column contains the filter list while the right column contains the filter preview and the Random Series and Random Episode buttons.

At the bottom of the window we have the User Toolbar which contains your server’s status on the left-side and user options on the right-side.

Filter Toolbar

As previously mentioned, the Filter Toolbar allows you to navigate, search and manage your collection and is composed of two parts, the Options Section and the Breadcrumb Section.


The Options Section is, as you may have guessed, a row of options that are used to navigate and maintain your collection.

OptionDescription
Back ButtonTake you back to the previous item on the breadcrumb bar.
Refresh ButtonRefreshes your entire collection, depending on your collection size it can take awhile to complete this task.
New Group Filter ButtonBrings you to the Create Group Filter window allowing you to input your filter’s information.
New Group ButtonBrings you to the Create Group window allowing you to input your group’s information.
Sort TypeProvides you with a array of sorting options to sort your collection.
Quick Sort ButtonWill autosort your collection by Sort Name alphabetically.
Search BarWill search through your collection for any Series/Groups containing the letters or words you’ve inputted.

The Breadcrumb Section tracks the path you take while browsing your collection. You can also click on each link in the breadcrumb to be instantly taken back to that section.

Filter Preview

The Filter Preview is your gateway into your collection, from it you can either select one of the pre-made filters, each with their own set of Filter Conditions or create your own filter with it’s own set of conditions. However before continuing on it's important that you understand what a filter is and how you can use them to your benefit.

What Are Filters?

Filters provided tighter and more detailed control over your collection by allowing you to organize your collection in a variety of ways to your liking. The conditions and operators you set are carried out at a series level but shown at a group level. For example, if you have a group that consists of three different series with two of those series being marked as complete, a filter setup to show only Completed Series will include this group because at least one series in the group is complete. This default behavior can be overwritten by enabling the Apply Filter At Series Level Instead Of Group Level setting which is explained below.

It’s important to remember that each filter you add will act as an AND operator meaning a series must pass all selected conditions to be added in the filter.
